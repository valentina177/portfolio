Chip Chomps Filter:

2024

Software: Meta Spark Studio, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

I conceptualized and brought to life an engaging interactive game tailored specifically for Facebook and Instagram filters. Drawing inspiration from the beloved Chip City brand, this innovative filter invites users to indulge in a virtual cookie-eating adventure. Players can delight in munching on cookies while collecting them, with the ultimate goal of achieving a rewarding discount coupon. It's not just a game; it's a delightful way to interact with the brand and unlock exclusive offers, making the experience both entertaining and rewarding for users.
